Welcome to Our School

Our History

Founded in 1950, our school has been committed to providing excellent educational experiences for over 70 years. We started as a small institution with only three classrooms and have grown into a comprehensive educational facility serving over 1,000 students annually.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.

Our Achievements

Over the years, our school has received numerous awards for academic excellence, sports achievements, and community service. In 2023, our school was recognized as a National Blue Ribbon School, a testament to our commitment to educational excellence and student success.

Message from the Principal
